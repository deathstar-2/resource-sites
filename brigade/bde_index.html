The Brigade

The Modular Combat Brigade in most modern militaries includes an array of combat and support companies and platoons organized within battalions that can conduct complex offensive and defensive operations for lengthy periods of time.

The term modular indicates that it is moderately self preserving. Common to most militaries, these brigades offer independent survivability of two to three weeks dependent largely upon the combat power of its adversary. Features separating itself from its subordinate, the battalion are listed below.

  • 1. General Officer or Senior Field Officer Designated Commander


  • 2. Military Staff


  • 3. Conduct Broad-Scale Military Operations
