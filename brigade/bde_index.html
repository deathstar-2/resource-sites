Quality Management

"Quality is never an accident. It is always the result of intelligent effort." - John Ruskin

The quality of a military determines the extent to which it can guarantee the liberties of the people for which it serves. A quality management system helps commanders and their organizations at-echelon measure the degree of quality as well as understand the level to which each measured factor is contributing to or detracting from the quality of the organization. Absent quality management, militaries can increasingly become costly to governments, and increasingly degrade their ability to serve as an effective defense.
